# Vin.ID — Beta Feedback Prompts

Use these to guide your feedback. You don't have to answer all of them — pick
what stood out. Send answers via **Settings → Send beta feedback** (type
**Beta test result** for an overall write-up; **Bug** / **Confusing** /
**Trust / privacy concern** for specific issues).

## Overall
1. **What was confusing?** Where did you hesitate or get stuck?
2. **What felt unnecessary?** Anything that seemed like clutter or extra steps?
3. **Was mobile easy to use?** Any cramped, cut-off, or hard-to-tap spots?

## Vehicle Passport
4. **Did you understand the Vehicle Passport?** Could you explain it to someone?
5. **Would you share this with a buyer?** Why or why not?
6. **What made it feel trustworthy — or not trustworthy?**
7. **Did you trust the Record Confidence Score?** Did its meaning come across
   (documentation quality, not vehicle condition)?

## AI (mock)
8. **What would you expect AI to do here?** For diagnosis and for document
   extraction — what would make it genuinely useful?
9. Was it clear these features are **mock/demo** right now?

## Trust & privacy
10. **Were the privacy warnings clear?** (document sharing, personal-info flags,
    export warnings, "not an official document" disclaimers)
11. Did anything make you worried about your data?

## The big one
12. **Would you use this before selling or buying a vehicle?** What would have to
    be true for you to rely on it?

## Anything else
13. The one thing you'd change first.
14. The one thing you liked most.
